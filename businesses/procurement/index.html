Our process

A controlled path from request to receipt.

  1. DefineConfirm specifications, quantity, budget, location and timing.
  2. SourceIdentify suitable vendors and obtain comparable commercial offers.
  3. EvaluateReview price, quality, lead time, documentation and delivery terms.
  4. CoordinateManage purchase, supplier communication, logistics and progress updates.
  5. Close outConfirm receipt, resolve discrepancies and complete records.
